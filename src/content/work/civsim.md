  A browser-based companion app for Civilization VI that turns an active game into a living civic atlas. Users can model their empire's regions, provinces, counties, religions, yields, and improvements, then simulate elections across national, regional, and provincial levels with dashboards, representative directories, chamber control, political trends, and optional local LLM-generated election narratives.
